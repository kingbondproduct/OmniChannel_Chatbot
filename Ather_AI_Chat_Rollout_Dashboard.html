Why this channel & this order
1
WhatsApp · Live agent
Jun 2026
Turns on human live chat on WhatsApp.
Live agent — the foundation every later bot escalates to.
Cheapest, fastest live-chat to stand up. First: no deflection is safe without a human fallback.
2
App · Post-ownership bot V1
Aug 2026 · −20%
AI resolves post-purchase tickets & calls in the App.
Post — sets the pattern App-Pre (7) & App-live-agent (6) reuse.
App is ~51% of contacts. Second: the single biggest deflection lever, fastest payback.
3
WhatsApp · Post-ownership → App
Nov 2026 · −20%
Routes WhatsApp post-sale chats into the App bot.
Post — reuses phase 2, no rebuild.
Volume too small for its own bot. Third: reuses a now-stable App bot for near-zero effort.
4
WhatsApp · Pre-ownership
Feb 2027 · −10%
First pre-sales / enquiry deflection bot.
Pre — first pre-ownership coverage in the program.
WhatsApp is the natural top-of-funnel. Fourth: pre-sales is lower value than post-sale.
5
Mobile web → WhatsApp
2027
Redirects all mobile-web chats to WhatsApp.
Pre, Post & Live agent — all served via WhatsApp.
Thin mobile-web volume; no dedicated bot. Fifth: a near-free win once WhatsApp covers 1–4.
6
App · Live agent (selected)
2027
Human handoff in the App for selected complex tickets.
Live agent — completes the App's Pre / Post / Live triad.
~33% of post-sale cases always need a human. Sixth: added after App-bot maturity.
7
App · Pre-ownership
2027
Pre-sales support bot inside the App.
Pre — on the App.
Captures in-App pre-purchase intent. Seventh: smaller than WhatsApp's top-of-funnel.
8
Desktop web · Pre + Post
2027 · −15% / −20%
Web bot covering pre and post on desktop.
Pre (−15%) & Post (−20%); live agent via redirect.
Smallest contact share. Last: lowest-ROI channel, after mobile-first is done.
Stand up human handoff first, then automate the App — our biggest contact channel.
